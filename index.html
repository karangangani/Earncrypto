FAQ

What is AETHER?+
AETHER is a reward-based mining platform where users earn AE tokens by watching ads and inviting friends.
How can I earn AE?+
You can earn AE by watching ads, inviting friends through referral links, and completing mining levels.
How many ads can I watch?+
Users can watch up to 50 ads every 12 hours. After the limit is reached, the ad counter resets automatically after 12 hours.
How does the referral system work?+
When someone joins using your referral link, you receive rewards and your mining level increases.
Why is my ad not loading?+
Sometimes ads may not load due to network issues or ad network delays. Please try again later.
When will withdrawals be available?+
Withdrawals will be enabled in future updates when the AETHER ecosystem is fully launched.
